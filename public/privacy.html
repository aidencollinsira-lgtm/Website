Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ALC Insurance ("ALC," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you visit this website or submit the quote request form. By submitting the form, you acknowledge this policy. If you do not agree with it, please do not submit your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit the quote request form, we collect the information you choose to provide, which may include:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (optional)
  • ZIP code
  • Age range
  • Coverage type you're interested in (e.g., individual/family health, Medicare, dental, life insurance)
  • Current insurance status (e.g., uninsured, insured and shopping, losing coverage soon)
  • Household size
  • Preferred day and time to be contacted

We also automatically collect limited technical information when you visit the site, such as IP address, browser type, and general usage data, through standard web server logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Connect you with a licensed insurance agent who can discuss coverage options, eligibility, and pricing;
  • Contact you by phone, text, or email as described in the consent you provided on the form;
  • Prepare and, if you choose to move forward, submit an insurance application or quote request to insurance carriers on your behalf;
  • Maintain records of your inquiry and our communications with you;
  • Improve and maintain this website.

We do not use your information to make any final insurance eligibility or coverage decisions; those decisions are made by the insurance carrier you apply with.

3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only as needed to provide the service you requested:

  • Licensed agents at ALC, who will contact you about the coverage you inquired about;
  • Insurance carriers, if you choose to proceed with a quote or application, so they can evaluate and process it;
  • Service providers who help us operate this website and store submissions (e.g., hosting providers), under obligations to protect your information and use it only for that purpose;
  • As required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of ALC, our customers, or others.

We do not sell your personal information to unrelated third parties, and we do not share it for unrelated marketing purposes without your separate consent.

4. Your Consent to Be Contacted

By checking the consent box and submitting the form, you agree to be contacted by phone, text, or email by a licensed ALC agent about insurance options, including through the use of automated dialing or texting technology where applicable. Consent is not a condition of purchasing any product or service. Message and data rates may apply.

You can withdraw consent or ask us to stop contacting you at any time by replying "STOP" to a text message, telling an agent directly during a call, or contacting us using the information in Section 9 below. We will honor opt-out requests within a reasonable time, generally within 10 business days.

5. Financial Privacy Notice (Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act)

Because we help arrange insurance products, certain information you provide may be considered nonpublic personal information ("NPI") under the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act. We collect NPI from the information you provide on our form and, where applicable, from the insurance carriers and agents we work with. We do not disclose NPI to nonaffiliated third parties except as permitted by law, such as to complete a transaction you request, or as described in this policy. We maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your NPI.

6. Your Privacy Choices and Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information, which can include the right to: know what information we have collected about you; request a copy of it; request that we correct or delete it; and opt out of certain sharing or "sale" of personal information (as those terms are defined under applicable state law). Some states also treat health-related information as "sensitive" data requiring your opt-in consent before we collect or use it, which you provide by submitting the form.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information in Section 9. We will verify your request and respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain the information you submit for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, including maintaining records of consent, complying with legal and regulatory recordkeeping obligations, and resolving disputes. When information is no longer needed, we take reasonable steps to delete or de-identify it.
